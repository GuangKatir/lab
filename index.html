A little about me

I am self-taught Kenyan painter and artist with over 5 years of expeerience in creative work. I have created paintings for both personal and commercial decorations. Below, you will find some of my best works, and projects that I really enjoyed working on.

Decorative Art

I create unique artistic pieces you can use to decorate your personal or professaional space. My decorative pieces focus on vintage and temporary themes.

Fine Art

Using paintings, I create fine art that focuses on presenting both the beauty of the world as we see it, and beauty as conceived by the mind.

Visual Art

Capitalizing on the power of the eye and mind, I draw paintings that bring you closer to nature, events, imaginations, and emotions, and the world.

The Rosy path

It was inspired by one of my visits to the scenic fields of Kiambu Country, on the northern side of the Aberdare slopes. On one sunny morning, the fields were very clear, with flowers spanning miles unending. The scenery was beckoning; urging me to pick up my brush and recreate it.
